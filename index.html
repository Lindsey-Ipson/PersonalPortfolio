About Me

I began teaching myself to code for fun and to nourish my passion for learning, and found I absolutely loved it. I’ve built web pages, applications, and small games, and what I’ve enjoyed most is the process of structuring smaller blocks of logic to build up larger, more complex code, and utilizing creativity, strategy, and resilience to work through any problems as they arise.
